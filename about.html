About

Our Origins

DoreanByte was founded by two close friends with the aim of developing intuitive, practical and straightforward software and hardware solutions. Predominantly a technology company, we saw that Malawi has a massive untapped potential as the next market for a digital business hub. DoreanByte aims to take advantage of this opportunity and create software ecosystems to cater to Malawi’s growing population and diverse needs.

Our Skills

Leveraging a diverse set of skills and expertise, DoreanByte aims to create solutions that tackle unique challenges present in society. All while creating an enjoyable and practical experience. DoreanByte also takes a stance on delivering, quality feature rich software. This is because we are passionate about providing our society with helpful solutions.

We have a particular knack for finding good solutions, we notice areas that are lacking in terms of useful technological services. We then work on conceptualizing and implementing solutions in the best way possible. We take great pride in our promise to quality, its safe to say we only associate the DoreanByte name with our finest work.

Our Goals and Aims

We are still young and though we are in our early stages of development, we have already proudly worked with Way Ministries. Designing and developing their website to help spread their message of teaching the next generation of Malawian leaders and thinkers.

With more projects to come. Apart from client fulfillment, we are also self-starting with our own projects that we are proud of as well. One of these is Katswiri, a job listing aggregator that helps pull content from various job listing providers and brings it all together in an intuitive and easy to use interface. This was done out of the passion of wanting to help job seekers and others in making it easier to find employment opportunities while reducing the mental overhead of having to search multiple job listing sources.

Our Moto

Our trait of not waiting for the solutions to problems from others in regards to the difficulties we notice in society by implementing our own answers is what makes us have an edge that we are proud of. We know our skill-sets and through DoreanByte we know we can achieve so much more. Malawi is growing, home to multiple industries and at the same time multiple problems. Where some see hopelessness we see opportunity, opportunity to impact people’s lives and go beyond by changing how we approach business, finance, health, productivity and so much more.

Imagination really is the limit and at DoreanByte, we are overflowing with positive and creative imagination that we want to share with the community. DoreanByte is already accomplishing goals and achieving some of our milestones. We work close with our clients, provide them with superb customer services and great solutions. We work close with ourselves creating an environment for growth and unleashing our creativity through our projects.
