App Development, Cross Platform and Native

Depending on your goals and needs we are able to leverage a variety of technologies that can bring native experiences for your users for example building Android applications with Kotlin/Java for the true native Android experience. But we are also able to leverage frameworks like Flutter with the Dart programming language or React Native if you as our client are seeking a multiplatform solution. On desktop we are also able to leverage the .NET framework to create native applications for Windows.

Our varied expertise makes it such that given your needs over the type of application you seek we can leverage the most optimal technology stack and utilise it to achieve your desired goals. We do our best to work with technology stacks we understand and are familiar with but are also open to expanding our skills by utilising recently developed frameworks as we know this is one of the ways we stay competitive.

We have had the pleasurable experience of developing our own Android application Katswiri using Flutter and are comfortable utilising it in future endeavours based on what you our client needs.
